About Romain

Photographer & Observer

A quiet dialogue between light, time, and emotion. I seek only to capture honesty in simplicity — the poetry of what’s real. I’m drawn to imperfection, to fleeting glances, to moments that seem to breathe even in stillness. My work remains minimal because meaning needs space. Every image I create is an invitation to pause, to feel something true, even when it can’t be explained.
